FOUNDER OF PISP

Making your Child's World Better

Angelina Y. Santos

Founder

It is my honor to extend to you a warm welcome to Pean Integrated School of Parañaque, as the founder of this distinguished institution. Our program is the culmination of a wealth of experience, time, and effort invested in its creation. The vision of PISP was born out of my own journey, which led me down this path. I faced a difficult decision between being a mother and a teacher, but my passion for teaching prevailed, and I established a tutorial center to serve the children of our community, welcoming them as they are.

At PISP, we are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and heard. We celebrate and appreciate differences in learning styles, recognizing that each child has unique potential. Our school creates meaningful interactions and encourages all students to engage in their learning experience. We believe that when students enjoy and feel comfortable with their teachers, progress follows naturally. Our students are encouraged to express themselves, as we cultivate an environment that promotes meaningful learning.

PISP is blessed with a team of committed and devoted individuals who share our vision and mission to nurture each student’s fullest potential. They have dedicated themselves to lifelong learning, ensuring that they are equipped to adapt to the changing world around us.

Looking back at our journey, I am overwhelmed with gratitude for the trust and confidence that the community has placed in our humble school. At PISP, we aspire to create a sense of belonging and acceptance in each student’s life, helping them recognize their place in this world and that they are valued for who they are.

Our success as a school is not measured by awards and accolades, but by the relationships that we create, forming deep bonds within the Pean community. We are proud to be a school that is not confined to typical expectations.

I invite you to take the time to know our teachers, alumni, and observe how we teach. Every child that comes to us experiences our love and care. You will witness the heart of the Pean community fighting for a better future.

In conclusion, I say that Pean Integrated School of Parañaque sees each student as a unique individual with great potential. Our aim is to make a difference in the lives of our students, and we endeavor to achieve this through unwavering commitment and compassion.

With warmest regards,
Angelina Y. Santos
"Founder of PISP"
